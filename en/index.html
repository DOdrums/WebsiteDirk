Dirk Ornee

Musician and Mixing Engineer

After studying jazz drums in Enschede, the Netherlands, I have been a gigging drummer for over 10 years. I have played with various bands and artists like Dual City Concert Band, Supermoon, Julie Huard, Millenium Jazz Orchestra and more. Currently holding down the drum chair with New Sound Jazz Machine and Trip the Light.
